Lead Follow-Up Automation & Assignment Optimization

  • Problem: Manual follow-up processes caused delays, and leads were not always assigned to the correct team, resulting in missed opportunities.
  • Solution: Implemented an automated follow-up process using Salesforce Flow that triggers email notifications to both the prospect and assigned sales team upon lead creation. Integrated Lead Assignment Rules and Queues to automatically route leads to the appropriate team based on criteria such as region and availability. Designed staged follow-up emails to ensure continuous engagement until the opportunity reaches Closed Won.
  • Tools: Flow Builder, Lead Assignment Rules, Queues, Validation Rules
  • Impact: Improved lead response time, ensured accurate lead distribution, and increased conversion rates through consistent follow-up.

Salesforce – Zuora Integration Optimization & Issue Resolution

  • Problem: Data mismatches between Salesforce and Zuora were impacting billing accuracy and causing inconsistencies in quotes used by sales and finance teams.
  • Solution: Analyzed and troubleshot integration issues by working with REST APIs to ensure proper data synchronization between Salesforce and Zuora. Identified and resolved discrepancies in quote and subscription data affecting downstream billing processes. Configured managed packages, optimized integration settings, and ensured proper data flow between systems.
  • Tools: REST APIs, SOQL (via Salesforce Inspector), Workbench, Managed Package Configuration, Integration Users, Permission Sets
  • Impact: Improved data consistency between systems, increased billing accuracy, and reduced errors impacting sales and finance operations.
